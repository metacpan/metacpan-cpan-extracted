AI/LLM Policy:

I will not police what tools you use to write code, including LLM.

I expect all PR's to be:
 * Reasonably sized
 * Human reviewable
 * Completely understood by the one submitting them
 * Documentation must not be "uncanny valley"

Clarification on docs: Documentation may be AI written, and may even be
identifiably so, but it must not feel unnatural to read.
