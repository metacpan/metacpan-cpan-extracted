=head1 NAME

Thunderhorse::Compatibility - Past and future compatibility notices

=head1 DESCRIPTION

Thunderhorse is B<currently in beta>. This page is a stub for compatibility
notices, which may start appearing after version C<1.000>. Until then, no
breaking of compatibility will be documented here.

Thunderhorse has a 2-year deprecation period policy on keeping compatibility,
excluding serious security fixes. Below are documented all future and past
breaking changes in the interface.
