=head4 Models

ID of the model to use. The options are C<gpt-4o-transcribe>, C<gpt-4o-mini-transcribe>, C<gpt-4o-mini-transcribe-2025-12-15>, C<whisper-1> (which is powered by our open source Whisper V2 model), and C<gpt-4o-transcribe-diarize>.

=over 4
      
=item * C<whisper-1>

=item * C<gpt-4o-transcribe>

=item * C<gpt-4o-mini-transcribe>

=item * C<gpt-4o-mini-transcribe-2025-12-15>

=item * C<gpt-4o-transcribe-diarize>

=back
