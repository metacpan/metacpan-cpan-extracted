Scan Frequencies

Click the "scan frequencies" icon in the toolbar to bring up the scan window.

The scan window allows you to re-scan the broadcast frequencies and updates the channel information. You only need to do this if a channel changes it's name or due to broadcast changes in your area.

Parts of the scan window

  • (A) The top shows the current progress as a progress bar and percentage
  • (B) The top right shows a "heartbeat". Every time the information is updated a new dot appears. This is used because the display may not otherwise visibly change.
  • (C) Under the progress bar is the frequencies list. The current frequency is shown in blue with a yellow border; a frequency that was tuned to is shown in green; a frequency that could not be tuend is shown in red.
  • (D) Under the frequencies are the list of found channels.

Scanning

Press the "scan" button to start scanning. The window will then show progress, the frequencies tuned to, and the list of discovered channels.
